# 1. Purpose

This document defines the phased roadmap for InsightFlow AI.

The roadmap outlines the evolution of the platform from a foundational analytics solution into a predictive and AI-driven decision intelligence platform for B2B SaaS organizations.

The roadmap is organized around business capabilities and value delivery rather than specific technologies.

-----------------------------------------------

# 2. Vision

InsightFlow AI aims to become a unified Customer, Subscription, Revenue, and Product Intelligence Platform that enables organizations to answer:

### Descriptive Analytics

What happened?

### Diagnostic Analytics

Why did it happen?

### Predictive Analytics

What will happen?

### Prescriptive Intelligence

What should we do?

-------------------------------------------------

# 3. Guiding Principles

The development and evolution of InsightFlow AI will be governed by the following principles:

### Business Value First

Platform capabilities shall prioritize measurable business outcomes including revenue growth, customer retention, operational efficiency, and executive decision support.

### Security by Design

Security controls shall be incorporated into every phase of the platform lifecycle rather than being implemented as an afterthought.

### Governance by Design

All phases of InsightFlow AI shall adhere to enterprise-grade governance standards including:

* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Data Classification
* Audit Logging
* Data Lineage
* Encryption at Rest and in Transit
* AI Governance and Explainability
* Regulatory Compliance Support

### Scalability by Design

The platform shall be designed to support increasing data volumes, users, business domains, and AI workloads without significant architectural redesign.

### AI Responsibility

AI capabilities shall be transparent, explainable, auditable, and aligned with responsible AI principles.

### Platform Agnosticism

Core platform components shall be designed to minimize cloud-provider lock-in where practical and support future deployment flexibility.

* MRR
* ARR

* Revenue Segmentation

### Subscription Lifecycle Intelligence

* New Subscriptions
* Renewals
* Upgrades
* Downgrades
* Grace Period Tracking
* Expirations

### Executive Intelligence

* Executive Dashboard
* Business KPI Monitoring

### Self-Service Analytics

* Business Reporting
* KPI Exploration

* Unified Revenue Visibility
* Unified Subscription Visibility
* Reduced Manual Reporting
* Trusted KPI Definitions

# 10. Phase 6 – Agentic AI

## Objective

Enable AI agents to investigate business issues and provide contextual explanations.

## Business Capabilities

### Revenue Investigation Agent

Analyzes revenue trends and identifies contributing factors.

### Churn Investigation Agent

Analyzes churn patterns and drivers.

### Customer Health Agent

Investigates customer risk indicators.

### Forecast Investigation Agent

Explains forecast changes and revenue risks.

## Example Questions

* Why did MRR decrease this month?
* Why are churn rates increasing?

## Expected Outcomes

* Faster Root Cause Analysis
* Improved Business Decision Support

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Phase 7 – Agentic AI Powered Decision Intelligence

## Objective

Enable AI-driven recommendations and decision support across business functions.

## Business Capabilities

### Executive Copilot

Provides executive summaries and strategic recommendations.

### Multi-Agent Orchestration

Coordinates specialized agents across revenue, subscriptions, customers, and forecasts.

## Example Questions

* What are the top business risks this month?

## Expected Outcomes

* Proactive Decision-Making
* Improved Business Agility
* Executive Decision Support

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Success Criteria

The roadmap will be considered successful when InsightFlow AI evolves from a reporting platform into a trusted decision intelligence platform capable of supporting descriptive, predictive, and AI-driven business decision-making.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Roadmap Governance

The roadmap will be reviewed at the end of each phase and may be adjusted based on:

* Business priorities
* Resource availability
* Technology maturity
* User adoption
* Stakeholder feedback
